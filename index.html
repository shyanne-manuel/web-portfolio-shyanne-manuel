Hey I'm Shyanne!

I've always been drawn to creativity and problem-solving. One of my ways to bring my ideas to life is through building visually appealing websites. My journey into web development started back in high school when I tackled my first-ever website for a project. I found this really cool animation that I wanted to try. At that point, the only thing I knew was the classic “Hello, World!” in raw HTML. But that wasn't going to cut it. I dove deep into research, figuring things out one step at a time until my vision became reality.

Fast forward a few years, and I decided to take things to the next level by enrolling in the Front-End Web Developer Program at BCIT. And now? Here I am, coding my way through ideas, making things happen, and always hungry to learn more.

I'm also passionate about storytelling through videography. I challenge myself to see the world through different perspectives, capturing the emotion and essence of a moment to amplify its impact. I had some experience covering weddings and with the help of my friends created short films and fun videos.
